About Knorr

Delivering flavor and nutrition to Filipino families for generations

Our Story

For over 180 years, Knorr has been at the forefront of flavor innovation and culinary excellence. What started as a simple endeavor to deliver quality food products has evolved into a global mission to make nutritious, delicious meals accessible to all.

In the Philippines, Knorr has become a trusted household name, helping families create memorable meals that bring people together. Our commitment to nutrition and sustainability drives everything we do.
