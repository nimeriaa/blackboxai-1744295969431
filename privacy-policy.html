Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Learn how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Knorr collects, uses, and protects any information that you provide when using our website. We are committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Name and contact information
  • Demographic information
  • Preferences and interests
  • Other information relevant to customer surveys and offers

How We Use Your Information

We use this information to:

  • Improve our products and services
  • Send promotional emails about new products or special offers
  • Customize the website according to your interests
  • Contact you for market research purposes

Security

We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. We have put in place suitable physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.

Cookies

A cookie is a small file that asks permission to be placed on your computer's hard drive. We use traffic log cookies to identify which pages are being used. This helps us analyze data about webpage traffic and improve our website.

Links to Other Websites

Our website may contain links to other websites of interest. However, once you have used these links to leave our site, you should note that we do not have any control over that other website.

Your Rights

You may choose to restrict the collection or use of your personal information. You have the right to request details of personal information which we hold about you.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time by updating this page. You should check this page regularly to ensure that you are happy with any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our Contact Page.
