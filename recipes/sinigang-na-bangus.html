Sinigang na Bangus

A sour and savory Filipino fish soup that's perfect for rainy days

Prep Time

20 mins

Cook Time

40 mins

Servings

5 people

Difficulty

Medium

Ingredients

  • 1 large bangus (milkfish), cleaned and cut into serving pieces
  • 2 packs Knorr Sinigang sa Sampalok Mix
  • 2 pieces tomatoes, quartered
  • 1 piece onion, quartered
  • 2 pieces eggplant, sliced
  • 200g kangkong leaves
  • 4 pieces okra
  • 2 pieces green chili
  • Fish sauce to taste

Cooking Instructions

  1. 1.

    Boil 8 cups of water in a pot.

  2. 2.

    Add onions and tomatoes. Cook until tomatoes are soft.

  3. 3.

    Add bangus and Knorr Sinigang sa Sampalok Mix. Simmer for 10 minutes.

  4. 4.

    Add eggplant and okra. Cook for 5 minutes.

  5. 5.

    Add kangkong leaves and green chili.

  6. 6.

    Season with fish sauce to taste.

  7. 7.

    Serve hot with steamed rice.

Chef's Tips

  • Clean the bangus thoroughly to avoid a fishy taste.

  • Add radish for extra flavor and nutrition.

  • Serve with patis (fish sauce) and calamansi on the side.

Nutrition Information
