Chicken Tinola

A comforting Filipino soup perfect for rainy days

Prep Time

20 mins

Cook Time

30 mins

Servings

4 people

Difficulty

Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into serving pieces
  • 2 Knorr Chicken Cubes
  • 2 green papaya, cut into wedges
  • 2 cups sayote, cut into chunks
  • 2 thumbs ginger, sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 cups malunggay leaves
  • Fish sauce and pepper to taste

Cooking Instructions

  1. 1.

    In a large pot, saut√© garlic, onion, and ginger until fragrant.

  2. 2.

    Add chicken pieces and cook until lightly browned.

  3. 3.

    Pour in 6-8 cups of water and add Knorr Chicken Cubes. Bring to a boil.

  4. 4.

    Add papaya and sayote. Simmer until vegetables are tender.

  5. 5.

    Season with fish sauce and pepper to taste.

  6. 6.

    Add malunggay leaves and cook for another minute.

  7. 7.

    Serve hot and enjoy!

Chef's Tips

  • For extra flavor, add lemongrass stalks while simmering.

  • You can substitute malunggay with spinach or other leafy greens.

  • Serve with steaming hot rice and calamansi on the side.
