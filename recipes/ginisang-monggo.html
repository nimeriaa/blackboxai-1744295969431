Ginisang Monggo

A hearty Filipino mung bean stew perfect for any day

Prep Time

60 mins

Cook Time

30 mins

Servings

4 people

Difficulty

Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mung beans (monggo), soaked overnight
  • 2 Knorr Pork Cubes
  • 250g pork belly, diced
  • 2 cups malunggay leaves
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cups spinach
  • Chicharon for topping
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Cooking Instructions

  1. 1.

    Boil mung beans in water until soft and mushy. Set aside.

  2. 2.

    In a pan, saut√© garlic and onion until fragrant.

  3. 3.

    Add pork and cook until browned. Add Knorr Pork Cubes.

  4. 4.

    Add cooked mung beans and simmer until the mixture thickens.

  5. 5.

    Add malunggay leaves and spinach. Cook until wilted.

  6. 6.

    Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  7. 7.

    Top with chicharon before serving.

Chef's Tips

  • Soak mung beans overnight to reduce cooking time.

  • Add ampalaya leaves for extra nutrition and authentic taste.

  • Serve with steamed rice and fried fish on the side.
